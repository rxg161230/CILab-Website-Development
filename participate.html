On-Going Investigations with Human Subjects in the CILab
All research studies are approved by the Institutional Review Board of The University of Texas at Dallas. See the flyer below for more information on subject eligibility, experience, and, compensation. For first time participants, please complete our eligibility survey so the CILab can make sure you are eligible to participate with us. Once this is completed, one of the CILab staff will get in contact with you and schedule your participation day. More information of each of the on-going studies can be found at links below. Each season, a participation calendar is generated for scheduling purposes. These are updated every participation season (2x a year).
