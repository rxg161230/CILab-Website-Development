Software programming for the CCi-MOBILE

The CCi-Mobile research platform comes with a software suite that enables researchers to conduct a wide range of experiments. There are example applications for sound processing (using ACE sound coding), audio capture and analysis, and to conduct psychophysics experiments out-of-the-box. The software is highly modular and reconfigurable, enabling researchers to edit/modify the code to fit their experimental needs. The software comes in two flavors. 1) PC-based software, which is developed in MATLAB and 2) Android-based software developed as Apps in JAVA.
